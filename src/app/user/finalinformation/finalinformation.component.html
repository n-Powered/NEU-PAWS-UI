New Amazon Users

Follow these steps to setup a new Amazon account in the Amazon Alexa app.
    1. Open the app and click “Create a new Amazon Account” to setup a new Amazon account.

    2. Complete  the registration field (ie: name, mobile number, password) and click “Submit.”

    (Note: For easier setup, we recommend creating the account with your Husky email address. If you choose not to, no problem, but using Paws will just require an additional step to link it with your Husky email. More on that below.)


    3.Enter the OTP that is sent to your mobile number, then click “Verify.”

    4. Follow the prompts from Amazon to setup the application on your device.

Link Paws to Your Husky Account

If your Amazon account email is not the same as your Northeastern Husky email address, and you would like to access your personal information through Paws, you’ll have to link those two accounts. Follow these last few steps to link them.

    1. Within the Paws skill, select “Settings”

    2. Select “Link Account”

    3. Type and submit your Husky email address to sign in

    4. Enter your myNortheastern username and password

    5. You’re ready to ask Paws!
