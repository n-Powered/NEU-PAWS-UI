Pick the data you want access to:

  • Key Contacts & Advisors

    This category will include name and contact information for your key contacts such as Academic, co-op, financial aid, or athletic advisors; Resident Assistant and Resident Directors, etc.

  • Important Documents, Dates, & To Do's

    This category will include acknowledgement of whether your financial aid documents are on file, health plan waiver or health insurance documents on file, etc. It will also include information about the status of your refund requests, payment plans, or I Am Here.

  • Balances, Dues, Financials

    This category will include information about your account balances, such as tuition, room and board balances, husky/dining, laundry and print dollar balances, etc. as well as fees assessed, such as library fees, etc.

  • Schedules & Important Dates

    This category will include information about your individual class schedule, class location, class time, name of professor/instructor, etc.

  • GPA and Grades

    This category will include information about your overall GPA and your final grade for a particular course.

  • Housing

    This category will include information about your housing assignment, room number, mailbox combination, housing selection information, move-in dates, etc.
