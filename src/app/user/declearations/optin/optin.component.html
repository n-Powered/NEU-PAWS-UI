Skill Data Opt-in

In order for the Paws Alexa skill to relay information to you through your Echo or the Alexa app, you must opt-in to thw type of data you wish to receive. Select from the categories below to enable them within the skill. You may return to this site to change your preferences at any time.

Key Contacts & Advisors

This category includes name and contact information for your key contacts such as Academic, co-op, financial aid, or athletic advisors; Resident Assistant and Resident Directors, etc.

Balances, Dues, Financials

This category includes information about your account balances, such as tuition, room and board balances, husky/dining, laundry and print dollar balances, etc. as well as fees assessed, such as library fees, etc.

GPA & Grades

This category includes information about your overall GPA and your final grade for a particular course.

Important Documents, Dates, & To Do's

This category includes acknowledgement of whether your financial aid documents are on file, health plan waiver or health insurance documents on file, etc. It also includes information about the status of your refund requests, payment plans, or "I Am Here".

Schedules & Important Dates

This category includes information about your individual class schedule, class location, class time, name of professor/instructor, etc.

Housing

This category includes information about your housing assignment, room number, mailbox combination, housing selection information, move-in dates, etc.
