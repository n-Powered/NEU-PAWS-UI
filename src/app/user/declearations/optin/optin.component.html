Skill Data Opt-in

In order for the MyHusky Alexa skill to relay information to you through your Echo device or the Alexa app, you must opt-in to the type of data you wish to receive. Select from the categories below to enable them within the skill. You may return to this site to change your preferences at any time.

Please consider that the information you consent to share with MyHusky will be available on all devices associated with the Amazon account you link. If other individuals have access to any of these devices, please consider creating a new Amazon account to link with MyHusky and associating it with personal devices that only you have access to.


The categories of information that I consent to make available to the MyHusky skill are:

Key Contacts & Advisors

This category includes name and contact information for your key contacts such as Academic, co-op, financial aid, or athletic advisors; Resident Assistant and Resident Directors, etc.

Balances, Dues, Financials

This category includes information about your account balances, such as tuition, room and board balances, husky/dining, laundry and print dollar balances, etc. as well as fees assessed, such as library fees, etc.

GPA & Grades

This category includes information about your overall GPA and your final grade for a particular course.

Important Documents, Dates, & To Do's

This category includes acknowledgement of whether your financial aid documents are on file, health plan waiver or health insurance documents on file, etc. It will also include information about and holds on your account, the status of your refund requests, payment plans, move-in dates, or "I Am Here".

Schedules & Important Dates

This category includes information about your individual class schedule, class location, class time, etc.

Housing

This category includes information about your housing assignment, room number, mailbox combination, housing selection information, etc.

Opt-out

By opting-out of all categories, none of your personal data will be available to MyHusky. Any data that may have been previously shared with the platform will be removed.
